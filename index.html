Information

No Solace is a small label/mailorder focused on Mgła, Kriegsmaschine and related projects, operated by the members of the bands. Primary aim is to allow for full catalogue of Mgła/KSM releases to be available, as well as releasing selected titles. No Solace is the main label for Kriegsmaschine. Northern Heritage is the main label for Mgła.
